objective: "Recreate a physical LEGO aircraft in CATIA by modeling individual sections of the set, assembling the complete design, and implementing modifications to improve the original configuration while maintaining compatibility with existing LEGO elements."
methods: "Grouped similar LEGO components and measured them with digital calipers, translating the measurements into individual CATIA parts using pads, pockets, and patterns before assembling the complete aircraft from stacked plate assemblies. This required interference checks and iterative adjustments to resolve overlapping geometry. Modifications were constrained to existing LEGO components and included a new nose wheel, a redesigned nose cone, and the removal of unnecessary studs to improve appearance and aerodynamic profile. The completed assembly was documented through engineering drawings and rendered alongside the physical model for comparison."
